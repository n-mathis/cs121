The Flower Finder Story...

By Alanna, Isaiah, Lizzy, and NaNa
Flower Finder is a web application built by computer science students Alanna DeMuro, Isaiah Fujii Bresnihan, Elizabeth Riffle, and NaNa Mathis. We were assigned to build an application with a deep learning backend for our Computer Science 121: Software Development course. We wanted to find a way to share our love of nature and flowers with the world. The goal was to make flower information accessible to all. So, we decided to build an application that is capable of recognizing 102 different flower types from a user uploaded image. We hope we are able to share our love of nature with you through this app!

To get started, go to the Idenitfy page and upload an image of a flower (.png, .jpg, or .jpeg). Once you click upload, you'll see which flower it is, as well as the second and third predictions. We also provide more information about the flower, like how to plant it and care for it at home. Start exploring!
